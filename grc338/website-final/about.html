About Us

Welcome to Tandoori Tales, a place where authentic Indian flavors meet creative innovation. Tandoori Tales was founded by two brothers, who grew up immersed in the rich culinary traditions of India. With a deep love for the bold, aromatic spices of their heritage, the brothers dreamed of sharing these unforgettable flavors with the world, all while putting their own modern twist on classic Indian cuisine.

The name Tandoori Tales was born from the brothers' desire to share the stories that come with every dish cooked in the tandoor. The tandoor is more than just a traditional cooking method–it's a symbol of India's rich culinary history, where meals are prepared slowly and with love. Each dish we serve is a "tale" of the vibrant flavors, ingredients, and techniques passed down through generations, and we're excited to tell those stories with every bite.

After years of experimenting with recipes and perfecting the art of tandoori cooking, the brothers set out to create a space that would bring together their shared passion for food and the spirit of Indian hospitality. From the smoky perfection of Tandoori Chicken to the unique fusion of Tandoori Chocolate Cake, every dish on our menu is crafted with care and love, combining time honored Indian recipes with new, exciting flavors.

From the heart of our kitchen to the warmth of our dining room, we invite you to join us and become a part of the Tandoori Tales family. Every meal is a chapter in the story we're telling, and we’re excited to share it with you.
