Introduction

Thillana

Thillana is a digital magazine dedicated to the rhythm, passion, and storytelling of Indian dance forms. This magazine explores the rich traditions of Bharatanatyam, Mohiniyattam, Kathakali, Odissi, and Bhangra by capturing its cultural significance and evolution through time. Altogether, Thillana brings to life the dynamic and diverse world of Indian dance, blending classical elegance with contemporary energy.
